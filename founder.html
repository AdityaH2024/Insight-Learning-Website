The Story

A Passion for Teaching,
A Mission for Results

Shailendra Rajput founded Insight Learning with a single powerful conviction: every student deserves access to world-class education, regardless of their background. Growing up in Pune, he witnessed firsthand how quality coaching could transform a student's future — and equally, how its absence could hold brilliant minds back.

After completing his degree with top honours in Mathematics and Physics, Shailendra Singh spent several years teaching at various coaching institutes across Pune. He quickly built a reputation for his exceptional ability to simplify complex concepts and genuinely connect with students. But he knew he could do more.

In 2008, he founded Insight Learning right here in Alandi — a place he calls home. Starting with just a handful of students in a small classroom, his institute grew rapidly through word of mouth, driven entirely by results. Today, Insight Learning has helped over 500 students achieve their academic dreams.
