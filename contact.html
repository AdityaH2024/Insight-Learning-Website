Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a demo class?
Fill the form above or call/WhatsApp us at +91 88066 05550. Our team will schedule your free demo within 24 hours.
What are the batch timings?
We offer Morning (7–9 AM), Afternoon (1–3 PM), and Evening (5–8 PM) batches. Specific timings vary by course and grade.
What is the batch size?
Maximum 20–25 students per batch to ensure personalised attention for every student.
Are study materials included in the fees?
Yes. All printed study notes, question banks, and practice papers are included at no extra charge.
Do you offer doubt-solving sessions?
Every course includes scheduled weekly doubt sessions. Students can also approach teachers directly after each class.
